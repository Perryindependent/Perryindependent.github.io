DENNIS AND KATHY DAVIS


BACK TO EMMANUEL

DENNIS DAVIS PASTOR OF EMMANUEL BAPTIST CHURCH HERE IN JACKSON
THEY HAVE TWO SONS ERIC AND DEREK.
DENNIS AND KATHY DAVIS SERVED AS MISSIONARIES TO THE COUNTRY OF VENEZUELA FROM 1980-1982.
THEY SERVED IN COSTA RICA FROM 1983 TO 1988. AND THEY SERVED IN MEXICO FROM 1989 TO 1990. SINCE THAT TIME THEY HAVE LIVED IN THE JACKSON TENNESSEE AREA. MEMBERS OF THE EMMANUEL MISSIONARY BAPTIST CHURCH.
