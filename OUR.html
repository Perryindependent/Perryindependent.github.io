WE ARE AN INDEPENDENT BAPTIST CHURCH


We are not affiliated with any denomination or organization.We are a self-governing church and not controlled by a religious hierarchy.

WE ARE MISSION MINDED

As commanded by our Lord we are striving to proclaim the gospel around the world as well as in our own community.

WE ARE A FRIENDLY CHURCH
